The Angels

Vanguard of the Expansionists

The Angel Units are the elite fighting force of the Resemblance. Crossing an Angel and coming back alive has never been done - New Eden uses its Angels to colonise smaller settlements out in the Resemblance which can be broken down to feed the engines of Industry. Angels are AMUs; this means they are robotic in nature. Their main processors are stored in the torso, alongside their WRITHE targeting system.
